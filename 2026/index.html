MMVE 2026

April 4th – 8th 2026 — Hong Kong SAR

Welcome to MMVE 2026

The International Workshop on Immersive Mixed and Virtual Environments Systems (MMVE 2026) will take place in person April 4th – 8th 2026 in Hong Kong SAR, co-located with the ACM Multimedia Systems Conference (MMSys'26). MMVE provides a discussion-focused forum between researchers and practitioners to exchange ideas, present advances in immersive media systems, and shape the future of XR research.

About MMVE

Since 2018, MMVE has evolved into a vibrant platform bridging XR technology, experience design, perception research, and systems engineering. The workshop welcomes contributions on immersive multimedia systems, user experience, performance and quality evaluation, accessibility, and emerging interaction paradigms enabling virtual, augmented, and mixed reality.

Topics of Interest
  • Immersive and Interactive Content
  • Quality Evaluation and System Performance
  • User Perception, Interaction & Behaviour in XR
  • Architectures for Immersive Multimedia Systems
  • XR Accessibility, Inclusivity, and Safety
  • Applications of XR in Public, Social, Cultural & Learning Contexts

We look forward to welcoming you in Hong Kong at MMVE 2026!
